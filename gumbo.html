cajun gumbo

ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken (use chicken from stock)

  • 900mls chicken stock

  • 175g frozen okra

  • 1 link chicken sausage

  • 10-12 king prawns

  • 1 diced green bell pepper

  • 2 finely diced celery stalks

  • 1 large diced onion

  • ½ cup flour

  • ½ cup oil

  • ½ cup chopped parsley

  • ½ teaspoon cayenne pepper

  • ½ teaspoon black pepper

  • 2 bay leaves

  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme

  • 2 teaspoons salt

  • 4 garlic cloves

  • 2 teaspoons paprika

  • 1 scallion

  • salt to taste


    • directions

    1. Starting with the roux, in a cast iron casserole or large pot combine flour and oil and stir for two minutes. This is the base of gumbo and must be cooked on low heat for an hour until it turns a deep brown. It is imperative to monitor the roux and stir at 1 minute intervals to avoid it burning. If it burns discard and re-start.

    2. While the roux forms, take the time to dice the vegetables, shred the chicken, chop the parsley and mince the garlic.

    3. Cut the sausage into even slices and fry them in a pan with oil until the pieces brown on both sides. This should take 5 minutes, once this is done set aside.

    4. After an hour, the roux should be ready, stir in diced onion, bell pepper and season with a teaspoon of salt leaving to simmer for 10 minutes.

    5. After 10 mins, stir in garlic, cayenne pepper, paprika and black pepper. Let simmer for 2 minutes

    6. Add chicken stock, bay leaves, thyme and parsley. Season with salt and leave to simmer for an hour while stirring occasionally

    7. After an hour add the chicken, and sausage to the pot and leave to simmer for 20 minutes again stirring occasionally

    8. Chop your okra while the pot simmers, stir the okra into the pot and let it simmer for another 20 minutes, stirring occasionally

    9. Add prawn to the pot and simmer for 15 more minutes again stirring occasionally

    10. Turn off the fire, salt to taste and put the pot aside, letting it sit for an hour before serving. Serve over warm rice and garnish with chopped scallion. Enjoy.
